# Development Roadmap

1. **Phase 1 — Foundation:** monorepo, UI shell, typed API, persistence plumbing, observability, tests, CI, and containers.
2. **Phase 2 — Data Intelligence:** governed ingestion, validation, profiling, feature contracts, and lineage.
3. **Phase 3 — Probabilistic Forecasting:** time-based evaluation, baselines, probabilistic models, and forecast APIs.
4. **Phase 4 — Trust and Conformal Calibration:** calibrated intervals, drift monitoring, model cards, and diagnostics.
5. **Phase 5 — Causal Intelligence:** causal assumptions, incrementality estimation, sensitivity analysis, and evidence.
6. **Phase 6 — Scenario Simulation:** versioned scenarios, uncertainty propagation, and comparison workflows.
7. **Phase 7 — Risk-Aware Optimization:** explicit constraints, robust objectives, and human-approved allocations.
8. **Phase 8 — RAG Copilot and Agent Orchestration:** grounded retrieval, citations, deterministic tools, and approval gates.
9. **Phase 9 — Deployment and Demo Hardening:** production data services, authentication, telemetry, scaling, and operational runbooks.
